# AI-Industry-Analytics-Dashboard
Developed an interactive AI Industry Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to analyze business performance across industries and regions. Designed KPI cards, bar charts, maps, donut charts, scatter plots, and slicers to visualize revenue, employee distribution, and geographic trends, enabling effective business insights and decision-making.
Developed an end-to-end AI Industry Analytics Dashboard in Power BI to provide comprehensive business performance analysis across industries and global regions. The solution incorporates data modeling, KPI development, interactive filtering, and advanced visual analytics to monitor revenue performance, workforce distribution, and geographic business presence. By leveraging maps, bar charts, donut charts, scatter plots, and dynamic slicers, the dashboard enables stakeholders to uncover actionable insights, compare industry performance, and support evidence-based business decisions through an intuitive and scalable reporting interface.
